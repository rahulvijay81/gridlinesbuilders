ABOUT US

In 2018, Grid Lines Builders was established. Our constructions and designs are based on the government's Kerala Municipality building regulations, which require final clearance from authorities. In the Tirur region, we have finished more than forty buildings and villas. We make sure that every stage of the building process goes without a hitch because to our diligent work ethic, unwavering attention to detail, and no-nonsense approach. Our top goals for your custom house are functionality and beauty, and we take great satisfaction in bringing your vision to life with a design that flawlessly captures your individual preferences, requirements, and way of life. We've built a solid reputation for making construction simple for our customers, and we're dedicated to providing a smooth home-building experience.
